News & Updates

Stay informed with the latest news, updates, and milestones from ASDY Foundation as we continue our journey toward achieving Zero Hunger.

June 2025 April 2025 March 2025 February 2025 January 2025

June 2025 — Launch of New Community Farming Project in Hambantota

We’ve officially launched our latest Community-Led Sustainable Farming Initiative in Hambantota. This program supports over 50 smallholder farmers with training, resources, and access to local markets. The project is expected to yield a 30% increase in crop productivity and improve nutrition for over 300 families.

April 2025 – School Nutrition Program Reaches 10,000+ Children

More than 10,000 nutritious meals have been provided to rural children through our School Nutrition Program. This initiative improves health and educational outcomes for underprivileged students.

March 2025 — Food Waste Reduction Campaign Launched

We kicked off our #NoMoreWaste campaign to reduce food waste at community and household levels. In partnership with local markets, we’ve introduced a redistribution program that redirects surplus food to families in need.

February 2025 — ASDY Joins UN Local Partnership Network

ASDY Foundation is now a proud member of the UN Sustainable Development Local Action Network, reinforcing our commitment to SDG 2: Zero Hunger. This partnership enables stronger collaboration with local and international NGOs.. This initiative improves health and educational outcomes for underprivileged students.

January 2025 — Impact Report Released

Our 2024 Impact Report is now available! Highlights include

15,000 meals distributed

200+ farmers trained

25 community gardens established

60% increase in household food security in target areas
