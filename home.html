WHO ARE WE

We are ASDY Foundation, a non-profit organization committed to eliminating hunger by promoting food distribution, sustainable agriculture, and community nutrition.

Our Mission & Commitment to SDG 2

At ASDY Foundation, we four university students turned co-founders, are deeply committed to advancing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger. What began as a university project has grown into our shared mission to help eradicate hunger and malnutrition by promoting food security, sustainable agriculture, and fair access to nutritious food for everyone.

We believe that access to sufficient and healthy food is a fundamental human right and the backbone of sustainable development. As young changemakers, we stand for the idea that real impact starts with determined youth. Every step we take is guided by our common purpose: to uplift vulnerable communities especially children, women, and smallholder farmers — by strengthening local food systems, supporting resilient agriculture, and reducing food waste.

Our journey together has shaped an approach rooted in community-led farming projects, nutrition education, food distribution, and partnerships with local organizations and governments. By working side by side with communities to build food security and sustainable practices, we hope to turn our vision four university students daring to dream of a hunger-free world — into a reality that makes lasting change.
